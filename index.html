CAIO GANAIS

I'm Caio Ganais, Brazilian and living in Spain since I was 10 years old. I hold a degree in Cross-Platform Application Development (DAM) and am currently studying Network Computer Systems Administration (ASIR).

IT is my professional safe harbor, a field where I find stability while exploring my passions. I'm fascinated by the world of hacking and cybersecurity, areas where curiosity becomes a fundamental tool.

I'm a music lover in the broadest sense: from forró and samba to Metallica and hard techno, I've attended festivals like Duro Festival and concerts ranging from Travis Scott to Jamiroquai. This same curiosity has led me to master five languages (Portuguese, Spanish, Catalan, English, and French) and to become passionate about art in all its forms: painting, cinema, photography, fashion, and philosophy.

I'm an all-rounder who finds in technology the perfect medium to materialize ideas and solve problems. I'm looking for projects where I can contribute not only technical knowledge, but also fast learning ability, adaptability, and diverse perspectives.

"Your new favorite Brazilian."
